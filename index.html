WHO ARE THEY?

DASH (they/them)

Born in the small town of Marari, Dash grew up surrounded by beaches and water. The legend of the dash stone was quite popular in the town and Dash grew up hearing the tale. According to the fable the dash stone was formed when a torpedo, stuck in a typhoon, decided to electrocute everything around it. As a result, the entire typhoon became electrically charged. A strangely rounded stone that was at the eye of the typhoon was hit with all this electrical energy which made it incredibly powerful. If a person were to lay their hands on it, it was believed that they would become extremely powerful. Everyone thought that this was just a story made up by people but Dash was certain that there was some truth to it. They believe that it was the strong belief of the existence of the dash stone that finally led them towards it.

WHAT CAN THEY DO?

  • Hydrokinesis

    Hydrokinesis is the metaphysical psychic ability to control and manipulate the movement of liquid water using the power of the mind. Dash is also capable of restricted atmokinesis.

  • Superhuman Abilities

    The Dash possesses strength greater than that of normal human beings. They also seem to possess superhuman durability, speed, hearing ability, maintain perfect bearings at all times as well as rapid healing abilities.

  • Zoolingualism

    Zoolinguism is the ability to communicate with animals. Dash uses this ability to communicate & forms telepathic bonds with various marine life and equestrian creatures easily.

  • Physiological Adaptations

    The Dash possesses a hybrid set of human lungs and aquatic gills enabling them to not only breathe on land but also underwater. Webbed hands and feet also develop on their person.
